About Us

Women in Procurement Finland ry is a registered non-profit association dedicated to promoting professional development, networking, and visibility in procurement and related fields (finance, supply chain, sustainability, IT) across Finland. The association especially supports individuals who identify as women or are committed to advancing gender equity and inclusive leadership within these professions.
