About WiP Finland ry

WiP Finland ry is a non-profit association dedicated to supporting women in procurement through mentorship, networking, and knowledge sharing. We believe in the power of community and collaboration to elevate careers and drive meaningful change in the procurement profession.

Founded by Katarzyna Fonteyn, WiP Finland ry brings together professionals from diverse backgrounds to foster growth, confidence, and leadership in sourcing, supply chain, and procurement roles.

Mentoring Program 2025–2026

This free mentoring program supports women in procurement in Finland by fostering community, growth, and mentorship. Whether you're a seasoned expert or early in your career, join us as a mentor or mentee to grow together.
