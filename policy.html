1 Purpose

WiP Finland ry is a non-profit association dedicated to fostering community, learning, and empowerment among women in procurement. This policy outlines the principles and expectations that guide the association’s activities, interactions, and partnerships.

2 Guiding Principles

  • Mutual Respect: Respect each other’s time, perspectives, and boundaries.
  • Confidentiality: Keep all shared experiences and discussions confidential at all times.
  • Inclusivity: Foster a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for learning and growth.
  • Integrity: Uphold ethical behavior with zero tolerance for misconduct.

3 Privacy Commitment

  • Handle personal information with care and discretion.
  • Use data solely for association-related purposes.
  • Store data securely and do not share with third parties without consent.
  • Comply fully with GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

4 Respect for Others

  • Treat others with kindness, professionalism, and respect.
  • Avoid discriminatory, harassing, or offensive behavior.
  • Support a culture of openness and constructive dialogue.

5 Confidentiality & Sensitive Information

  • Do not disclose sensitive or personal information at any time.
  • Do not use confidential information for personal, professional, or commercial gain.
  • Do not record or distribute content without explicit permission.

6 Commercial Use & Sponsorship Policy

  • Association activities are strictly non-commercial.
  • No marketing, sales, or promotional use is permitted.
  • Sponsorships are welcome if aligned with values and mission.
  • Sponsors may contribute financially or in-kind but not for direct advertising or commercial gain.
  • All sponsorships require board approval.

7 Anti-Bribery Policy

  • Zero tolerance for bribery and corruption.
  • Uphold the highest standards of integrity.
  • Report unethical behavior to the board.

8 Membership & Participation Policy

Eligibility: Open to individuals who support the mission of empowering women in procurement.

Membership Types:

  • General Members
  • Mentoring Participants
  • Board & Volunteers

Participation Expectations:

  • Engage respectfully and professionally.
  • Follow association policies and values.
  • Do not use platforms for personal or commercial promotion.

Code of Conduct:

  • Respect diversity and differing viewpoints.
  • Maintain confidentiality and privacy.
  • No harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior.

Termination: Membership may be revoked for violations or misuse.

Joining & Leaving:

  • Register through official channels.
  • Additional registration may be required for specific programs.
  • Members may leave at any time by notifying the board.
  • Membership fees are non-refundable upon leaving.
  • No prorated fees or discounts for mid-term joining.

9 Enforcement & Reporting

Violations may result in removal. Report concerns confidentially to the board.

10 Association Term

The association term is from October 1st to September 30th.
