1 Guiding Principles

  • Mutual Respect: Members and participants are expected to respect each other’s time, perspectives, and boundaries.
  • Confidentiality: All shared experiences and discussions are to be kept confidential at all times.
  • Inclusivity: The association aims to create a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for learning and growth.
  • Integrity: Ethical behavior is expected at all times, with zero tolerance for misconduct.

2 Privacy Commitment

  • Handled with care and discretion
  • Used solely for association-related purposes
  • Stored securely and not shared with third parties without consent
  • Managed in full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws

3 Respect for Others

  • Treat others with kindness, professionalism, and respect
  • Avoid discriminatory, harassing, or offensive behavior
  • Support a culture of openness and constructive dialogue

4 Confidentiality & Sensitive Information

  • Disclose sensitive or personal information shared within the association at any time
  • Use any confidential information for personal, professional, or commercial gain
  • Record or distribute any content from sessions or communications without explicit permission

5 Commercial Use & Sponsorship Policy

  • No individual or organization may use association programs, events, or communications for marketing, sales, or promotional purposes.
  • Sponsorships are welcome if they align with the association’s values and mission.
  • Sponsors may contribute financially or in-kind, but must not use the association for direct advertising or commercial gain.
  • All sponsorships require board approval to ensure alignment with the association’s commitment to integrity, inclusivity, and community-building.

6 Anti-Bribery Policy

  • Uphold the highest standards of integrity
  • Avoid offering, giving, or receiving any form of bribe or unethical incentive
  • Report any suspected unethical behavior to the board immediately

7 Membership & Participation Policy

Eligibility

Membership in WiP Finland ry is open to individuals who support the association’s mission of empowering women in procurement. Participation in programs and events is voluntary and inclusive, regardless of professional background, age, or experience level.

Membership Types

  • General Members
  • Mentoring Participants
  • Board & Volunteers

Participation Expectations

  • Actively engage in association activities with respect and openness
  • Follow the association’s policies and values
  • Communicate respectfully and professionally with others
  • Refrain from using association platforms for personal or commercial promotion

Code of Conduct

  • Respect for diversity and differing viewpoints
  • Commitment to confidentiality and privacy
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior

Termination of Membership

  • Violates the association’s policies or code of conduct
  • Engages in behavior that harms the community or its reputation
  • Uses the association for unauthorized commercial or promotional purposes

Joining & Leaving

  • Members may join by registering through official channels
  • Participation in specific programs may require additional registration or commitment
  • Members may leave the association at any time by notifying the board or program coordinator

8 Enforcement & Reporting

Violations of this policy may result in removal from the program or association. Concerns or breaches should be reported confidentially to the board for review and appropriate action.
